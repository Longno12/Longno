Our Mission

At PillowTalk, we're on a mission to amplify the voices of the RecRoom community. We believe in the power of conversation to connect, entertain, and inform. Our goal is to create a platform where the most important people in RecRoom - you, the players - can share your stories, insights, and experiences.

Through engaging discussions and interviews, we aim to showcase the diversity, creativity, and passion that make RecRoom such a vibrant virtual world. PillowTalk is more than just a talk show; it's a celebration of community and a bridge between players, creators, and developers.

Meet Our Team

Behind PillowTalk is a dedicated team of RecRoom enthusiasts who are passionate about creating quality content for our community. Each member brings unique skills and perspectives, united by our love for RecRoom and its players.

From our hosts who guide the conversations to our behind-the-scenes crew who ensure everything runs smoothly, we're committed to delivering episodes that are insightful, entertaining, and reflective of the diverse RecRoom community we serve.
