01. About Me

Hello! I'm Joe (Longno), a 19-year-old cybersecurity enthusiast from England. My journey in tech started in software development, where I quickly learned that an application is only as strong as its weakest link. This realization pivoted my focus towards the offensive side of security.

Today, my work revolves around penetration testing, vulnerability research, and promoting secure coding practices. I thrive on the challenge of discovering exploits before malicious actors do, whether through rigorous testing, code analysis, or participating in CTFs and bug bounty programs.

My philosophy is simple: security isn't a feature, it's a foundation.
